  1. What is the probability that we can observe a less or equal signal like of 6800 cosmic rays given there's one cosmic ray per minute and a the moon is observed 8 hours a day for 15 days?
  1. mean of # of cosmic rays over 15 days is 1608*15 = 7200 and we know that cosmic ray has poisson distribution

We can then take the integral from 0 to 6800 over the pdf of poisson distribution with a mean of 7200 to get the probability for = 7200

p(k) =$\int_0^{6800} e^{-}\frac{^k}{k!}dk$
